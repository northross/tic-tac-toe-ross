Tic-Tac-Toe

Rules of the game..............................................

1. The game is played on a grid that's three squares by thtree squares. 2. You are X, your 'friend' is O. 3. Alternating turns, select a square to place your mark. 4. The first player to get three of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. 5. When all nine squares are full, the game is over. 6. Play again. 7. Was there a tie? Play best out of three!
